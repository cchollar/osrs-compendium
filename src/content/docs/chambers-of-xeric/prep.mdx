### General CM Layout Prep Procedure
This is a list of steps for prepping supplies for yourself after Shamans in a Challenge Mode layout raid. These steps will start upon entering the farming room directly after Shamans. These steps can also be adapted to team raids, if prepping.

:::note
This particular prep procedure relies on using the potion secondaries from Ice Demon, prepping a total of 8 Xeric's Aids, 3 Revitalizations, and 1 Prayer Enhance. Ensure when starting prep that you have at least secondaries for those potions as well as the Buchu seeds dropped by Shamans in the room prior if you are planting Buchu yourself.
:::

<Steps>

1. Take a `Spade` from the pile of `Old Tools`.

2. Plant a Buchu seed (if team hasn't planted for you).

3. Path to storage chest.

4. Drop all secondaries and spade.

5. Click `Private` on the storage chest, and Deposit-All

6. Pick up dropped secondaries

7. Click `Pick-lots` on the Gourd Tree.

8. Click `Use` on the Geyser, dropping 12 empty gourd vials as you make your way to it.

9. After filling up the remaining 12 gourd vials, path to grown Buchu plant and start harvesting, cleaning the herbs as you harvest

10. Path towards the next room that leads down to Vanguards, making your potions as you path there

11. Continue pathing towards the storage chest that is in the room, continuing to make whatever potions are needed.

12. When at the storage chest, drop leftover secondaries and spade on the floor to clear inventory, click `Private` on storage chest

13. Withdraw-all gear

14. Deposit 5 Xeric's Aids into the storage chest, leaving 3 in the inventory

15. Withdraw remaining gear needed for upcoming rooms (lockpick, etc)

16. Make your way to Vanguards while reorganizing your inventory as desired

</Steps>
